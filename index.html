Who we are

TMS

Trust Manpower Supply (TMS) is a leading recruitment agency approved by the Supreme Committee and Legacy based in the State of Qatar. Established in 2008 as part of Al Jaber Group. We have built an enviable reputation of providing our clients with the most qualified personnel for their manpower needs.

We are providing top-notch and globally competitive services in overseas, local, and household recruitment.

We utilize multiple methods of attracting qualified candidates including headhunting, database search, and advertisement in local and international newspapers, and through our networking partnership with strategically located associate agencies.

We provide solutions which are unique and unmatchable in the industry while thriving to help you in meeting your business objectives.
